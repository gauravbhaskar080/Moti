ACCESS TO CULTURAL AND SPORTS ACTIVITY AT NATIONAL LEVEL: Kendriya Vidyalaya not only offers inter class competitions, but also organises Cluster, Regional and National level competition for Indoor/Outdoor sports, Creativity, And cultural activities as well.
